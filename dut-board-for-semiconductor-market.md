<p><p>DUT boards in the semiconductor market come in three primary types: Needle Type, Vertical Type, and MEMS Type. Needle Type DUT boards utilize needles to establish contact with device pins, ensuring high precision in testing. Vertical Type DUT boards are designed for efficient space utilization and allow easier access to components during testing. MEMS Type DUT boards are specialized for testing micro-electromechanical systems, incorporating sensitive components and features to evaluate the performance of these advanced technologies effectively.</p></p>
