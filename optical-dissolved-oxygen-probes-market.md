<p><p>Optical dissolved oxygen probes are classified into three market types based on their wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire. </p><p>- **2-Wire Probes** use basic wiring for power and signal transmission, making them simple and cost-effective, suitable for standard applications. </p><p>- **3-Wire Probes** provide enhanced signal stability by incorporating a separate ground, reducing interference. </p><p>- **4-Wire Probes** offer the highest accuracy with independent channels for power and signal, ideal for demanding environments requiring precise measurements. Each type balances performance, complexity, and cost based on specific application needs.</p></p>
