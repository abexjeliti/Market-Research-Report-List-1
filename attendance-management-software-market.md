<p><p>Attendance management software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based attendance management software is hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing users to access the system via the internet. This type offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. Conversely, on-premise attendance management software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type offers unique advantages depending on organizational needs.</p></p>
