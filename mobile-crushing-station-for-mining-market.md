<p><p>The mobile crushing station for the mining market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Cone Crushing Station**: Utilizes a rotating cone to crush materials, ideal for secondary and tertiary crushing, offering high efficiency and fine product quality. </p><p>2. **Jaw Crushing Station**: Features two jaw plates to compress and fracture materials, known for its robust construction and suitability for primary crushing.</p><p>3. **Impact Crushing Station**: Employs impact force to break rocks, effective for producing sand and gravel with a high cubicity and particle shape. Each type serves distinct crushing needs in mining operations.</p></p>
