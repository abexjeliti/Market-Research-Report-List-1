<p><p>The financial planning software market comprises two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based financial planning software allows users to access their data and tools remotely via the internet, facilitating collaboration, scalability, and real-time updates. It often operates on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premise financial planning software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization. However, it typically requires more significant upfront investment and IT resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
