<p><p>The Flow Controllers market is divided into two main types: volumetric flow and mass flow. Volumetric flow controllers measure the volume of fluid passing through a system, commonly used in applications requiring precise liquid or gas management. In contrast, mass flow controllers determine the mass of the fluid, offering greater accuracy for applications involving varying densities. Each type serves specific industries, emphasizing accuracy and efficiency in processes such as manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
