<p><ul><li>Titanium Oxide Type</li><li>Zirconia Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle oxygen sensor market is primarily divided into two types: titanium oxide and zirconia sensors. Titanium oxide sensors operate on catalyst principles, effectively measuring oxygen levels through temperature changes, making them ideal for specific applications in fuel efficiency and emissions control. Conversely, zirconia sensors utilize a solid-state electrolyte to detect oxygen concentration changes, providing precise readings in exhaust systems and enhancing engine performance. Both types are crucial for optimizing catalytic converter functionality and reducing harmful emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
