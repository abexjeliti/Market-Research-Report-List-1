<p><ul><li>Dry Prepreg Cloth Carbon Fiber</li><li>Wet Prepreg Cloth Carbon Fiber</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone carbon fiber market is categorized into three primary types: dry prepreg cloth carbon fiber, wet prepreg cloth carbon fiber, and others. Dry prepreg cloth carbon fiber involves the use of resin-infused fabrics that cure without additional solvents, offering lightweight and durable solutions for phone casings. Wet prepreg cloth carbon fiber uses a liquid resin process, allowing for more complex designs and better adhesion. The "others" category includes alternative composites and hybrid materials, catering to specific performance needs and aesthetic preferences in mobile phone manufacturing.</p></p>
