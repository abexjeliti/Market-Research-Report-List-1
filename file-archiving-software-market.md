<p><p>File archiving software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based archiving offers scalability, remote access, and reduced infrastructure costs by storing data on third-party servers. It is ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and ease of management. In contrast, on-premises archiving involves installing software on local servers, providing complete control over data and compliance with security regulations. This option is preferred by businesses with stringent data governance requirements and existing IT infrastructure.</p></p>
