<p><strong>The Reflex Type Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retroreflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflex type photoelectric sensor market includes two main types: retroreflective and diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors. Retroreflective sensors utilize a reflector to bounce light back to the sensor, allowing for longer detection distances and improved accuracy, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, diffuse reflective sensors detect objects by emitting light that gets reflected off the target back to the sensor itself, offering a more compact design ideal for shorter ranges and versatile mounting options in various environments.</p></p>
