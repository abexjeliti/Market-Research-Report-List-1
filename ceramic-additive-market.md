<p><p>The ceramic additive market is segmented into several types, including dispersants, lubricants, grinding aids, and others. Dispersants help improve the flow and stability of ceramic slurries, ensuring uniform distribution of particles. Lubricants reduce friction during the shaping and processing of ceramics, enhancing mold release and surface finish. Grinding aids facilitate the grinding process, improving efficiency and product quality by minimizing energy consumption and preventing the agglomeration of particles. The "others" category includes various niche additives that cater to specific applications within the ceramic industry.</p></p>
