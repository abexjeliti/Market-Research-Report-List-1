<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry pump market consists of various types tailored for diverse applications. Centrifugal pumps efficiently move fluids through rotational energy, ideal for high flow rates. Positive displacement pumps provide precise dosing and are suitable for viscous substances. Jet pumps utilize high-speed jets to create suction, effective in low-pressure environments. Air lift pumps use compressed air to transport liquids, often in bioreactors. Other pumps encompass specialized designs that cater to unique pharmaceutical processing needs, ensuring reliable and sanitary fluid transfer.</p></p>
