<p><p>The SLI (Starting, Lighting, Ignition) battery market includes three primary types: Flooded Batteries, AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Batteries, and Others. Flooded batteries are traditional, featuring liquid electrolyte and requiring maintenance. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, offering enhanced safety and performance with minimal maintenance. The "Others" category encompasses niche technologies like gel batteries and lithium-ion options, which are emerging for specific applications due to their distinct advantages in weight and lifespan. Each type caters to different automotive needs.</p></p>
