<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat-film extrusion lines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their configuration. Vertical lines utilize a standing setup, allowing gravity to assist in the processing and cooling of films, which enhances efficiency with reduced floor space. Conversely, horizontal lines feature a lengthwise design, providing more control over film thickness and surface quality. Each type serves distinct applications in packaging, automotive, and other industries, catering to varying production needs and material specifications.</p></p>
