<p><p>The Trauma Fixation Product Market is categorized into several types. Internal fixation includes groups like plates, which provide stable bone support; screws, which secure bone fragments; and nails, used for intramedullary fixation of long bones. Other internal fixation methods encompass various devices for specific applications. Conversely, external fixation involves frame or pin systems that stabilize fractures from outside the body. Each type is designed to promote optimal healing and alignment of fractured bones, catering to distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
