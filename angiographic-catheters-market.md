<p><ul><li>Cutting Balloon Catheters</li><li>Drug-eluting Balloon Catheters</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheters</li><li>Conventional Balloon Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiographic catheters market includes various types designed for specific therapeutic functions. Cutting balloon catheters are equipped with tiny blades to facilitate tissue dilation and minimize vessel damage. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release medication to prevent restenosis during angioplasty. Scoring balloon catheters feature scoring elements to create micro-tears in the plaque for easier dilation. Conventional balloon catheters are standard devices used to expand narrowed vessels. Each type plays a critical role in interventional procedures and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
