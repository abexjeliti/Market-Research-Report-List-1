<p><ul><li>Diffusion Type</li><li>Pump Suction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Formaldehyde Tester market is primarily categorized into two types: diffusion type and pump suction type. Diffusion type testers operate by allowing air to passively enter through a membrane, where formaldehyde reacts with a sensor, providing an instantaneous measurement. In contrast, pump suction type testers actively draw air samples into the device using a pump, ensuring faster and more controlled sampling. Both types are essential for monitoring formaldehyde levels in various environments, including industrial, residential, and healthcare settings.</p></p>
