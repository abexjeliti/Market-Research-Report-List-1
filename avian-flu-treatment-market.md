<p><p>The avian flu treatment market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Antibiotics** are used to prevent secondary bacterial infections in infected birds, although they do not treat the viral infection directly.  </p><p>2. **Vaccines** aim to immunize poultry against avian influenza strains, reducing disease incidence.  </p><p>3. **Antiviral agents** work to inhibit the replication of the virus, improving recovery odds.  </p><p>4. **Immunoglobulins** provide passive immunity by transferring antibodies, offering immediate protection to affected birds. Each type plays a critical role in managing avian flu outbreaks.</p></p>
