<p><p>An inverted bucket steam trap is a device used to remove condensate and air from steam systems while preventing the loss of live steam. It consists of a buoyant bucket that floats on the condensate, allowing it to open and close the discharge port based on the water level. This design effectively manages condensate drainage in steam applications, ensuring optimal efficiency and energy savings.</p><p>The inverted bucket steam trap market is experiencing significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
