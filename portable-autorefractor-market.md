<p><p>A Portable Autorefractor is a compact, lightweight device used to measure the refractive error of the eye quickly and easily. They provide rapid, accurate assessments of an individual's vision without needing a full eye exam.</p><p>The Portable Autorefractor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period. The trend towards telehealth and remote patient consultations has also spurred demand for portable devices, as they facilitate eye examinations in various settings, including schools and community health clinics. Additionally, innovations in technology, such as improved optics and enhanced user interfaces, are making these devices more attractive to healthcare providers.
