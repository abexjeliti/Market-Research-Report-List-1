<p><strong>The Electrical Explosion Proof Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame-proof Type</li><li>Increased Safety Type</li><li>Intrinsic Safety Type</li><li>Positive-pressure Type</li><li>Oil-immersed Type</li><li>Sand Filled Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical explosion-proof equipment market consists of various types designed to ensure safety in hazardous environments. Flame-proof type equipment contains explosions within the enclosure. Increased safety type reduces the risk of ignition through enhanced design. Intrinsic safety type limits electrical energy to prevent ignition. Positive-pressure type uses overpressure to keep hazardous gases out. Oil-immersed type submerges components in oil to prevent sparks. Sand-filled type encases equipment in sand to absorb explosions. Other types may include a combination or specialized designs for unique applications.</p></p>
