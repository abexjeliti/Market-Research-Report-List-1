<p><p>The pharmaceutical borosilicate glass tubes market is categorized primarily into two types: alkaline borosilicate glass tubes and neutral borosilicate glass tubes. Alkaline borosilicate glass tubes contain additional alkaline materials, enhancing chemical durability but making them reactive to certain substances. In contrast, neutral borosilicate glass tubes are designed to minimize reactions with pharmaceuticals, providing superior stability and safety for sensitive applications. Both types are critical for packaging and delivering medications in the pharmaceutical industry, ensuring product integrity and efficacy.</p></p>
