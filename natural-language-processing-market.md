<p><ul><li>Rule-Based</li><li>Statistical</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) market types include Rule-Based, Statistical, and Hybrid approaches. Rule-Based systems rely on predefined linguistic rules and dictionaries to parse and understand text, offering transparency and control but limited flexibility. Statistical methods, conversely, utilize algorithms and large datasets to identify patterns in language, enabling adaptability and improved accuracy but potentially lacking interpretability. Hybrid models combine both strategies, leveraging the strengths of rule-based systems and statistical approaches to enhance performance, offering a balanced solution for diverse NLP applications.</p></p>
