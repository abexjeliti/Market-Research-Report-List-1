<p><ul><li>Unimorph</li><li>Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric discs market is primarily segmented into unimorph and bimorph types. Unimorph piezoelectric discs consist of a single active piezoelectric layer bonded to a passive substrate, allowing for bending and deflection under an electric field. In contrast, bimorph discs are constructed from two piezoelectric layers, enhancing their actuation capabilities by creating a larger displacement. Both types are utilized in various applications, including sensors, actuators, and energy harvesting devices, driven by their precise motion control and responsiveness to electrical signals.</p></p>
