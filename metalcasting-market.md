<p><strong>The Metalcasting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metalcasting market encompasses various techniques used for shaping metals. Gravity casting relies on gravity to fill molds, ideal for large components. High-pressure die casting uses high-pressure machines to inject molten metal quickly, ensuring precision and short cycle times, commonly used in automotive parts. Low-pressure die casting involves introducing metal under low pressure, offering good surface finish for larger items. Sand casting utilizes sand molds, suitable for complex shapes and low-volume production, making it versatile for various applications.</p></p>
