<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring devices encompass various types used to assess heart function over time. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recordings typically over 24-48 hours, capturing arrhythmias during daily activities. Event monitors are patient-activated devices that record heart activity during specific symptoms, used for longer monitoring periods. Mobile cardiac telemetry offers real-time data transmission for continuous, remote monitoring, detecting irregularities instantly. Other market types may include wearable devices and implantable loop monitors, expanding options for cardiac assessment and patient convenience.</p></p>
