Top loader machines feature a lid on the top for loading clothes, offering convenience and faster wash cycles. They generally use more water and energy. Conversely, front loader machines have a door at the front, known for their energy efficiency and gentler washing action, making them ideal for smaller spaces. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact appliances in urban settings.</p></p>
