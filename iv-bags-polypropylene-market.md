<p><p>The IV bags polypropylene market is primarily segmented into two types: PP-H (Polypropylene Homopolymer) and PP-R (Polypropylene Random Copolymer). PP-H is recognized for its high rigidity and crystallinity, making it suitable for applications requiring mechanical strength and heat resistance. In contrast, PP-R offers enhanced flexibility and lower temperature sensitivity, making it ideal for applications that require lower intravenous infusion rates. Both types are valued for their chemical resistance, lightweight properties, and compatibility with a variety of medical solutions.</p></p>
