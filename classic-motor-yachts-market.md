<p><ul><li>Displacement Hull</li><li>Planing Hull</li><li>Semi-Displacement Hull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Classic motor yachts are categorized into three main market types based on hull design. Displacement hulls move through the water efficiently at slower speeds, ideal for long-distance cruising. Planing hulls are designed for higher speeds, gliding over the water's surface, suitable for performance-oriented boats. Semi-displacement hulls blend features of both, allowing for moderate speeds while maintaining some efficiency in calmer waters. Each type serves different boating preferences, from leisurely voyages to faster travels, catering to various market demands.</p></p>
