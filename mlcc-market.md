<p><p>The MLCC (Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor) market is categorized into several types based on dielectric materials and temperature characteristics. C0G (NP0) is stable across temperatures, ideal for precision applications. Y5V has high capacitance but significant changes with temperature and voltage, making it less reliable.
