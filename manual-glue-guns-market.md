<p><p>The manual glue guns market is primarily divided into two types: hot melt glue guns and cool melt glue guns. Hot melt glue guns use high temperatures to melt adhesive sticks, providing strong bonding for various materials, including wood, plastic, and fabric. They are favored for projects requiring quick setting times. In contrast, cool melt glue guns operate at lower temperatures, making them suitable for delicate surfaces and minimizing heat damage. This diversity allows users to select based on specific project needs and material compatibility.</p></p>
