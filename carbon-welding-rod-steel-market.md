<p><p>The carbon welding rod steel market is categorized into three main types: low-carbon steel, carbon steel, and high-carbon steel. Low-carbon steel contains a small amount of carbon, resulting in increased ductility and weldability, making it ideal for applications like structural projects. Carbon steel, with moderate carbon content, offers a balance of strength and toughness, suitable for general construction. High-carbon steel, containing higher carbon levels, provides exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making it appropriate for specialized applications like cutting tools and heavy machinery components.</p></p>
