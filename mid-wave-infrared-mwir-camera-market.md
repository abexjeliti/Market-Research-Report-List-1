<p><p>The Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) camera market is divided into two main types: cooled and uncooled cameras. Cooled MWIR cameras use cryogenic cooling systems to lower the temperature of infrared detectors, enhancing sensitivity and image quality, making them suitable for applications such as military surveillance and scientific research. Uncooled MWIR cameras, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, offering a more compact, cost-effective solution for applications like industrial monitoring and building inspections, albeit with reduced sensitivity compared to cooled versions.</p></p>
