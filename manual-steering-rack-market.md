<p><strong>The Manual Steering Rack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion Type</li><li>Ball Screw Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual steering rack market is primarily categorized into two types: rack and pinion type and ball screw type. The rack and pinion type utilizes a gear mechanism to convert rotational motion into linear motion, providing precise steering control. In contrast, the ball screw type employs a ball nut and screw system, which offers smoother operation and reduced friction, enhancing steering efficiency. Both types are integral to vehicle handling, with distinct advantages that cater to varying performance and design requirements.</p></p>
