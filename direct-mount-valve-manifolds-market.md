<p><p>The direct-mount valve manifolds market includes various configurations tailored for specific applications. Two-way manifold valves control the flow in a single direction, making them ideal for simple on/off operations. Three-way manifold valves can redirect flow between two outlets, offering more versatility in routing. Five-way manifold valves expand functionality further by allowing multiple connections and flow paths, making them suitable for complex systems requiring precise flow management. These types enhance efficiency and streamline installation in industrial and hydraulic applications.</p></p>
