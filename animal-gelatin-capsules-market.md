<p><p>The animal gelatin capsules market is segmented into three main types based on the source of gelatin: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin is derived from cattle and is widely used due to its versatile properties and compatibility with various formulations. Porcine gelatin, sourced from pigs, offers distinct advantages in terms of gelling strength and clarity, making it popular in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries. Piscine gelatin, obtained from fish, is increasingly favored for its halal and kosher certifications, appealing to specific consumer segments.</p></p>
