<p><p>The Liquid Macrofiltration market encompasses various filtration technologies tailored for different applications. Granular micro filters utilize granular media to remove particles, while filter press filters employ pressure to separate solids from liquids. Leaf tubular and press filters are efficient for high-viscosity fluids. Bag filters provide easy maintenance for solid-liquid separation, and drum and disk filters utilize rotating components for continuous filtration. Belt filter presses combine mechanical pressure and gravity, and AWB filters are designed for advanced wastewater treatment, enhancing process efficiency.</p></p>
