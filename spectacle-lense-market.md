<p><p>Spectacle lenses are optical devices used in eyewear to correct vision impairments such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Additionally, technological advancements in lens manufacturing, such as digital surfacing and customized designs, are enhancing optical performance and driving consumer interest. The expansion of online retail channels for eyewear is also reshaping market dynamics, offering convenience and competitive pricing.</p><p>The Spectacle Lens Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period, fueled by these trends and the increasing prevalence of digital device usage, leading to higher demand for specialized lenses that address modern vision challenges.</p></p>

<p><p>The spectacle lens market is primarily divided into two types: single vision lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses are designed to correct a single field of vision, either for distance or near sight. They are often prescribed for patients with specific visual needs. In contrast, progressive lenses offer a seamless transition between multiple vision zones, catering to individuals with presbyopia who require assistance at various distances. This diversity in lens types addresses different visual requirements, enhancing user comfort and functionality.</p></p>
