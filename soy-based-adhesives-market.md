<p><p>The soy-based adhesives market primarily consists of two types: ordinary soy-based adhesives and water-resistant soy-based adhesives. Ordinary soy-based adhesives are derived from soy protein and are primarily used for general bonding applications in industries like woodworking and packaging. In contrast, water-resistant soy-based adhesives are formulated to withstand moisture, making them suitable for products exposed to humidity or liquids, such as outdoor furniture and construction materials. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional synthetic adhesives, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
