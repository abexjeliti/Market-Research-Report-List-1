<p><ul><li>Swivel Caster</li><li>Rigid Caster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture casters market is primarily divided into two types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters allow for multidirectional movement, making them ideal for items requiring maneuverability, such as office chairs and mobile shelving. They consist of a wheel mounted on a rotating fork. In contrast, rigid casters provide linear movement and are fixed in one direction, typically used in stationary furniture like workbenches or heavy storage units, where stability and support are essential.</p></p>
