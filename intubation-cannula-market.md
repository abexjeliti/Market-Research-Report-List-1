<p><strong>The Intubation Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tracheostomy</li><li>Nasopharyngeal</li><li>Oropharyngeal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intubation cannula market consists of three primary types: tracheostomy, nasopharyngeal, and oropharyngeal cannulas. </p><p>Tracheostomy cannulas are used for long-term airway access through an incision in the trachea. Nasopharyngeal cannulas are designed for temporary airway support, inserted through the nose to the pharynx, facilitating ventilation in emergencies. Oropharyngeal cannulas are also temporary devices, inserted through the mouth to maintain airway patency during unconsciousness or sedation. Each type addresses specific clinical needs in airway management and respiratory support.</p></p>
